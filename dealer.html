Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum investment required? expand_more
The minimum investment varies by city tier (Tier 1/2/3). Generally, it ranges from ₹5 Lakhs to ₹15 Lakhs, covering stock, shop branding, and working capital.
Do I need prior experience in the paint industry? expand_more
While experience in hardware or construction is beneficial, it is not mandatory. We provide comprehensive training for you and your staff on product technicalities and sales.
How do I get marketing support for my local shop? expand_more
Upon approval, USHA PAINTS provides standardized Glow-sign boards, interior branding materials, color shade cards, and catalogs free of cost. We also fund 50% of local newspaper or radio advertisements for authorized dealers.
What is the delivery turnaround time? expand_more
With our strategic regional depots, we maintain a 24-48 hour delivery timeline for all major cities and up to 72 hours for remote locations.
